Manual vs. Automated Garage Doors

Feature / Benefit Manual Operation Automated Conversion
Opening Mechanism Physical lifting and manual locking handles. Remote control fob, wall switch, or smartphone app.
Security Protection Basic locking bars vulnerable to tampering. Automatic motor locking with rolling-code signal encryption.
Safety Systems Relies fully on manual control and operational springs. Built-in obstacle detection auto-reverses if blocked.
Weather Convenience Must leave the car to unlock and open in bad weather. Drive straight inside without opening your car door.
