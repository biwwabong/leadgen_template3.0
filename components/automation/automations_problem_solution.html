Upgrade Your Manual Garage Door to Electric Automation

The Daily Inconvenience

  • Heavy, Strained Lifting: Battling a stiff, poorly balanced manual door in the rain or cold weather.
  • Security Vulnerabilities: Basic manual handles and external locks are easy targets for forced entry.
  • Physical Strain: Constant bending down and lifting heavy panels can risk back fatigue or injury over time.

The Automated Upgrade

Most existing up-and-over or sectional garage doors can be retrofitted with a high-torque, modern electric motor. Automation turns your current setup into a secure, remote-controlled entrance, giving you immediate access at the press of a button without stepping out of your vehicle.
