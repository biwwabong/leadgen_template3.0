Garage Door Installation FAQs

How long does it take to supply and fit a new garage door?

Once your custom site survey is complete and your door is manufactured, the physical on-site installation process typically takes between 3 to 5 hours. The team will fully test the tracking balance and operations before signing off the job.

Will you take away and dispose of my old garage door?

Yes. Vetted independent installation professionals handle the complete structural dismantling, removal, and environmentally responsible disposal of your old timber, steel, or fiberglass door as part of the standard service quote.

Do new garage doors come with structural warranties?

Absolutely. Every new garage door installation carries a comprehensive manufacturer's structural warranty covering the door panel integrity, mechanical parts (springs/cables), and electronic motor drive systems for total peace of mind.
