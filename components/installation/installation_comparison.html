Choosing the Right Garage Door Style

Compare the key differences between roller, sectional and up‑and‑over doors to find the perfect fit for your home.

Door Style Space Efficiency Insulation & Thermal Performance Best Suited For
Aluminium Roller Maximum: Rolls vertically into a compact box with no outward swing. High‑performance double‑skinned insulated slats. Short driveways, maximising ceiling space, modern automated convenience.
Insulated Sectional Excellent: Rises vertically then stores flat along the ceiling. Premium: Thick interlocking panels deliver the highest thermal seal. Integral garages, premium security, modern architectural homes.
Classic Up‑and‑Over Moderate: Swings outward before sliding back into the ceiling. Standard single‑skin panels (basic insulation). Budget‑friendly replacements, classic detached garages.
