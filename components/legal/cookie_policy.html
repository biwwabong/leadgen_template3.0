Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users. This helps us provide you with a smooth experience when you browse our site and allows us to make ongoing performance improvements.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or the hard drive of your computer or mobile device when you visit web pages.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the secure and basic operation of our web forms.
  • Analytical Cookies: Allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors and see how traffic moves around our platform so we can optimise layout structures.

3. Managing Your Settings

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept them, but you can usually modify your individual browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.
