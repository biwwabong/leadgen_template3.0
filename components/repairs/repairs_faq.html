Garage Door Repair FAQs

Can I safely replace a snapped garage door spring myself?

No. Garage door springs operate under immense mechanical tension. Attempting to unwind or replace them without professional specialized tools can lead to sudden, violent structural failures resulting in severe personal injury. Always leave spring work to experienced local technicians.

Why is my garage door opening crookedly or hanging to one side?

This is almost always caused by a snapped or severely frayed steel lifting cable on one side of the door, or uneven tension across the top spring assembly. Operating the door in this state will twist the structural tracks, so it should remain closed until a professional inspects it.
