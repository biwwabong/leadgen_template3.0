Garage Door Repair FAQs

Common questions homeowners ask before booking a mechanical garage door repair.

Can I safely replace a snapped garage door spring myself?

No. Garage door springs operate under immense mechanical tension. Attempting to unwind or replace them without specialist tools can cause sudden, violent failure and severe injury. Always leave spring work to trained local technicians.

Why is my garage door opening crookedly or hanging to one side?

This is almost always caused by a snapped or severely frayed lifting cable on one side of the door, or uneven tension across the spring assembly. Operating the door in this condition can twist the tracks, so keep it closed until a professional inspects it.
