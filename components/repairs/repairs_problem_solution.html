The Risks of a Broken Door

  • Trapped Vehicles & Assets: A snapped spring or frozen cable locks your car inside, disrupting your entire day.
  • Severe Safety Hazards: A garage door weighing over 60kg with failing lifting mechanisms can drop suddenly, causing catastrophic injury.
  • Open Security Risks: If the door is jammed half-open, your home, tools, and property are completely exposed to intruders.
