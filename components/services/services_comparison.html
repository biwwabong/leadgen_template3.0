Choosing the Right Service for Your Garage Door

A quick comparison to help you decide whether you need a repair, a full installation, or an electric upgrade.

Service Stream Primary Objective Typical Cost Frame Turnaround Time
Mechanical Repairs Fixes broken cables, snapped springs, jammed tracks or faulty locks. Restores full structural and mechanical function without replacing the door. Low to Moderate (Fixed local rates) Same‑Day: 1–2 hours on‑site completion.
Electric Automation Retrofits a high‑torque electric motor to your existing manual door, providing smooth, remote‑controlled operation with modern safety sensors. Moderate (Upgrade investment) Fast: 2–3 hours installation & configuration.
Full Installations Complete removal of the old door, site preparation and custom fitting of a brand new insulated roller or sectional door for maximum security and kerb appeal. Higher (Long‑term property value) Scheduled: 3–5 hour installation window.
