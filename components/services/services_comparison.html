Choosing the Right Service for Your Garage Door

Service Stream Primary Objective Typical Cost Frame Turnaround Time
Mechanical Repairs Fixes broken cables, snapped springs, jammed tracks, or faulty locks. Restores existing structural function. Low to Moderate (Fixed rates) Same-Day: 1–2 hours on-site execution.
Electric Automation Retrofits a high-torque remote-controlled electric motor track to your existing, smoothly operating manual door. Moderate (Upgrade investment) Fast: 2–3 hours system configuration.
Full Installations Complete site survey, structural removal of old door, and custom fitting of a brand new insulated roller or sectional door. Higher (Long-term asset value) Scheduled: 3–5 hours installation window.
