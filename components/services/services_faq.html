How do I know whether to repair my old door or buy a new one?

If your frame is square, structurally sound and free from deep rust or rot, a mechanical repair (springs, cables or rollers) is usually the most cost‑effective option. If the panels are warped, uninsulated or heavily damaged, a modern replacement offers far better security, insulation and long‑term value.
