How do I know whether to repair my old door or buy a new one?

If your door frame is structurally sound, square, and free of deep rust or rot, a simple mechanical repair (replacing springs or cables) is highly cost-effective. However, if the tracking panels are severely warped, uninsulated, or compromised, a modern replacement installation provides vastly superior security and thermal value over time.
