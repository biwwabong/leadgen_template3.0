Garage door not opening or closing properly?

Local engineers in Peterborough deal with these issues every day:

  • Snapped springs and worn cables: If your door feels heavy, drops suddenly, or won’t stay open, the springs or cables may have failed. A qualified garage door technician can replace these safely and rebalance the door.
  • Jammed tracks and misaligned rollers: Sticking, grinding or a door that jumps off its tracks usually means the rollers or tracks need realignment or replacement — often a quick repair without needing a new door.
  • Electric motor and remote control faults: Doors that stop halfway, don’t respond to the remote, or behave unpredictably can often be fixed with motor servicing, sensor checks or replacement control units.
